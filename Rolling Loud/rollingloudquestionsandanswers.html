COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS

Do minors need to be accompanied? Yes, those under 18 need a legal adult guardian.
What is the best plan to attend all three days? It is bet for attendees to plan ahead by booking hotel rooms and getting proper transportation to the site.
Can we bring food? Yes, food is permitted.
Is smoking and drinking allowed? Smoking and drinking are permitted on site grounds.
What is the festival like? The festival will be held in an open area with little area to sit or solicit. There will be restrooms but no guarentee of other resources.
